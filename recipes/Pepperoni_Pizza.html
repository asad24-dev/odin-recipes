Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Delicious pepperoni pizza with a crispy crust and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Flour (for dusting)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni slices and sprinkle Italian seasoning.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Enjoy your delicious pepperoni pizza!
