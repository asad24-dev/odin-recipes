Burritos

Description

Delicious burritos filled with beans, rice, and your choice of protein.

Ingredients

  • Burrito tortillas
  • Refried beans
  • Cooked rice
  • Grilled chicken or beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Warm the burrito tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  2. Spread refried beans on the center of each tortilla.
  3. Add cooked rice, grilled chicken or beef, and shredded cheese.
  4. Top with sour cream, salsa, guacamole, lettuce, and tomatoes.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll it up tightly.
  6. Heat in a skillet for a few minutes to crisp the tortilla.
  7. Slice in half and serve with extra salsa and guacamole.
  8. Enjoy your delicious burritos!
