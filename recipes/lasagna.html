Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It is typically baked in the oven until hot and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook the ground beef until browned.
  4. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
